Polygonal Geometry: Elementary School in Berlin

more about

The positioning and polygonal footprint of the new after school care centre at the Maria Montessori Elementary School in Berlin takes into account various different parameters confining the building site, as well as the relationship to the existing listed school building. The course of a 100-metre tartan track continues uninterrupted past a specially recessed main entrance area. The track connects the old schoolyard with the "garden theatre" created on the other side of the new structure.
